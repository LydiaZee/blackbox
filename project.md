# cleaning service app

## overview

it is for individuals and companies that need cleaning services. we are looking at hotels, BNBs,offices, apartments.Service providers offer  cleaning services to the vast number of customers.Customers have the right to choose who over they want to do their services. Customers are able to do comparison of prices

## customers
Create their usernames and passwords to join the application. They are able to set their preferences what type of cleaning service they are looking for

## supplier
 create their username and password to join the application. they input their prices for customers to choose from

 ## service menu
suppliers can add their prices. 
$25 per hour
$25 and above per hour
less than $25 per hour

## availability calender
suppliers mark their days of work
monday to sunday
8am-4pm
4pm-12am
12am-8am

## admin
they can approve supplier and customer accounts, remove suppliers and customers

# payment system
 payments shall be made using the following
 paypal
 debit/credit card
 apple pay

 customers to pay before the service

 ## ratings and reviews
 5 star rating system is used
 5 stars being the best 
 1 star being the worst or least

 suppliers with the highest ratings shall be used  shown more frequently


 ## chat system
 suppliers and customers can chat on the application.
customers can ask questions, leave cleaning instructions and send locations
